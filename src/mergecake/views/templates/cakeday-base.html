Cakedays

When registering to bring cake or another treat, you will be informed on submitting if there is a conflict with a colleague who has already committed to bringing cake on the requested date. You will then have to choose another day.

Successfully registered cakedays will result in an e-mail being sent out to your colleagues at 10:00 on the date chosen. This is to ensure that you follow through with your commitment.
